  • About the Developer

  • OpporWeb is developed by Oishani Ganguly. She's a student at Cornell University graduating in 2022 with a Bachelor's in Information Science. She is currently concentrating in Data Science and Interactive Technologies. She has a keen interest in Web Design with working knowledge in both. This website is a pet project and aims to alleviate the troubles college students encounter while looking for summer programs by having to scour the internet to find the a suitable project. With a wide range of listings on the website, OpporWeb hopes to overcome a part of this problem. Oishani hopes to expand the scope of the website beyond the CIS department and Cornell in the near future.
